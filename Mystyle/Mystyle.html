My style philosophy

I have always believed that minimalism is the way to go in terms of style. It is classy. It is timeless. It is essential. Style should deliver a statement without being overbearing. It should exude a quiet confidence by the wearer. Think of it like a well-crafted tool, I believe that style should be something that anyone can pick up and do great things with it. It is something that everyone can appreciate, no matter the background.

My environment

I live in Singapore. It is hot and humid here throughout the year, with temperatures ranging from 25 to 32 degrees celsius. It rains often too.

My take on style

I tend to style with as little layers as possible due to the hot weather here all round. My style is akin to those summer styles in other temperate countries.

My go-to colours

My skin undertone is warm. So I normally go for warmer colours when choosing my clothing. I tend to wear contrasts(white, grey, and blacks) and blues currently. I tend to be drawn to yellow, red, and greens.
